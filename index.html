SPELLS

  1. Expecto Patronum
    2. Expecto Patronum Patronus Charm

    <ecks-PECK-toh pah-TROH-numb>

    A highly powerful and advanced protective spell which will conjure a spirit guardian of wizard's positive emotions to defend against dark creatures; it can also send messages to other witches or wizards. The Patronus takes the form of an animal, unique to each person who casts it. The form of a Patronus can change when one has undergone a period of heightened emotion.
  2. Wingardium Leviosa
    3. Wingardium Leviosa Levitation Charm

    <win-GAR-dee-um lev-ee-OH-sa>

    Makes objects fly, or levitate. Taught in early first-year charms classes; this came into good use in later years by the main characters.
  3. Avada Kedavra
    4. Avada Kedavra Killing Curse

    <ah-VAH-dah keh-DAV-rah>

    One of the three Unforgivable Curses.Causes instantaneous death. It is accompanied by a flash of green light and a rushing noise. There is no known counter-curse that can protect the victim from dying, except for a loving sacrifice. Harry Potter survived from this spell couple of times. Used by Tom Riddle to murder many of his victims without remorse.
  4. Expelliarmus
    5. Expelliarmus Disarming Charm

    <ex-PELL-ee-ARE-muss>

    Forces whatever an opponent is holding to fly out of their hand. It was considered to be Harry Potter's signature spell. Used by Severus Snape on Gilderoy Lockhart during a live duelling demonstration during the first and last meeting of the Duelling Club in 1992.
  5. Lumos
    6. Lumos Wand-Lighting Charm

    <LOO-mos>

    Illuminates the tip of the caster's wand, allowing the caster to see in the dark. Used by Albus Dumbledore to illuminate the dark cave in 1997.

    Lumos Maxima

    <LOO-mos Ma-cks-ima>

    Produces a blinding flash of bright white light from the tip of the wand. It is a variant of the Wand-Lighting Charm.
